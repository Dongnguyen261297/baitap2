Bitcoin

Bitcoin (symbols: BTC, XBT, ) is a cryptocurrency , invented by an anonymous individual or organization using the name Satoshi Nakamoto as open source software [8] since 2009. [9] Bitcoin can be exchanged directly using an Internet-connected device without going through an intermediary financial institution . [ten] Bitcoin works differently than typical currencies: there is no central bank that regulates it, and the system is based on a peer-to-peer Internet protocol. [11] Bitcoin supply is automatic, limited, divided according to a predetermined schedule based on algorithms . Bitcoins are issued to Bitcoin "mining" computers as compensation for verifying Bitcoin transactions and recording them in a distributed ledger in a peer-to-peer network, via blockchain technology . This ledger uses Bitcoin as the unit of accounting. Each bitcoin can be divided into 100 million smaller units called satoshis. [4]

History

background

Bitcoin was designed by Satoshi in 2007 when he believed that it was possible to design a transaction system where members did not need to trust each other. The domain bitcoin.org was registered on August 18, 2008. [17] Bitcoin was first mentioned on October 31, 2008 in the peer-to-peer payment protocol prospectus [ 18] by the anonymous figure Satoshi Nakamoto . [19] It began to be used on January 9, 2009 with the creation of the Bitcoin genesis block. The following paragraph is embedded in the content of the origin block: The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks - Quote to a Times of London article pointing out that the British government has failed to stimulate the economy and is preparing to offer a package Second bailout for banks, caused by the fractional reserve banking system.
